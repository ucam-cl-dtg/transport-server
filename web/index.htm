OmniBus.

Britain's Public Transport Data made accessible

For developers

The United Kingdom's public transport data is notoriously dispersed and difficult to access. OmniBus will be a central access point for all of Britain's live public transport data.

Using OmniBus your can develop applications that take advantage of public transport data like never before.

For transport operators

Coverage

OmniBus currently covers every bus stop in Cambridgeshire.

Who runs OmniBus

OmniBus is the product of a summer research project by David Tattersall, at student at the University of Cambridge Computer Laboratory. The Computer Laboratory continue to run OmniBus and we're working hard at bringing more data providers to the service.

Thanks to Alastair Beresford, Andy Rice and all participating councils and transport operators.
